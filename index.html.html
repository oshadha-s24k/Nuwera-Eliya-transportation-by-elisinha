Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka's premium hill country station and former colonial health resort, benefits from a temperate climate of brisk sunny days, cooler nights and seasonal rains and is surrounded by verdant scenery of forests and tea gardens..

bomburu waterfall

Bomburu Falls is a waterfall located in the Uva-Paranagama Divisional Secretariat Division of Sri Lanka. It is located close to the borders of Nuwara Eliya and Badulla districts, about 15 km from Welimada town. Bomburu Falls is considered to be the widest waterfall in Sri Lanka. Bomburu Falls is also known as Perawella Falls..
